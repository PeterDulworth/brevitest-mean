Order a Test

Ordering a Brevitest test is easy, and you can order one from your desktop, tablet, or smartphone. Click on the button below, select the assay you want to run, enter a couple of other pieces of information, and your test will be saved in the cloud, ready to run!

Order

Run a Test

Once you've entered in the necessary information to set up the test, click on the Run button below. You'll be asked to choose the device you want to use, and then guided through a series of easy steps. Before you know it, you'll be running a Brevitest!

Run

Monitor Tests in Progress

While your tests (that's right, tests plural, you can run tests on several devices simultaneously) are running, you can check on their progress. Click the Monitor button below, and you'll be taken to a control center that displays real-time information on your Brevitest devices.

Monitor

Review Test Results

Getting test results is kinda the point of this whole exercise, isn't it? That's why Brevitest provides a simple way to get you test results, in a clean, sophisticated user interface. Get numerical and graphical results displayed on your desktop, tablet, or smartphone, wherever you are!
