Prescribe Tests

Prescribing a Brevitest™ is easy, and you can order one from your desktop, tablet, or smartphone. Click on the button below, select the assays you want to run, enter a little more information, and your prescription will be saved in the cloud, ready to run!

Prescribe

Run a Test

Once you've entered in the necessary information to set up the test, click on the Run button below. You'll be asked to choose the device you want to use, and then guided through a series of easy steps. Before you know it, you'll be running a Brevitest™!

Run

Monitor Tests in Progress

While your tests (that's right, you can monitor multiple tests) are running, you can check on their progress. Click the Monitor button below, and you'll be taken to a control center that displays real-time information on your Brevitest™ devices.

Monitor

Review Test Results

Getting results is kinda the point of this whole exercise, isn't it? That's why Brevitest™ provides a simple way to review your test results, in a clean, sophisticated user interface. Get numerical and graphical results whenever you want, wherever you are. Brevitest™ makes it easy!
